Why I Believe in God

I believe in God because the alternative is that everything that exists does so eternally and for no reason whatsoever. If that is the case, then the laws of nature and the fundamental constituents of reality must exist as unexplained brute facts. I find this explanation less likely than the alternative.

I think the ultimate explanation of reality is more likely to be intelligent rather than unintelligent, since an eternal mind provides a less arbitrary stopping point than eternal, purposeless laws. An eternal mind makes more sense to me as an explanation for the complex system that we find ourselves in, rather than that unintelligent complex system simply existing. That eternal mind is God.
